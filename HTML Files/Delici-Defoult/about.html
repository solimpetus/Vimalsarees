About Us

who we are

Nirmal Creations is an ode to the Indian tradition and culture. We glorify the tradition in our wide range of ethnic collection from sarees, lehengas, gowns and tunic - all under one roof.

ABOUT US

First &

The Finest

Nirmal Creations opened its new venture in the year 1998 and opened its store in Topsia, Kolkata. We spread our wings all over the local market and also cater to other parts of Kolkata as well. From the time of establishment till date, Nirmal Creations focus on customer satisfaction and gratification. We aim at nurturing our creativity and add more innovative angles towards each product and services every single time to quench the needs of our beloved customers.
With time and trend, the type of product we produce has adapted new innovations. From the initial stage to the present time, we always work for bringing for better. With time, the style, fashion, trend, everything has achieved new definition that definitely gets reflected in all our products. We aim at revering Indian traditions through our ethnic products from lehengas, to sarees, to gowns, to tunics and what not. Every attire speaks elegance. Keeping in mind about the current trends and tastes of customers, we try to inculcate tradition and modernity through every single product we cater.
